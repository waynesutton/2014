Hello! I hope you are enjoying the summer and life is amazing on your end. This is my third and most exciting update so far.



Since March I’ve been focused on using my best skills and passion while pursuing my dreams to continue to have an impact in the technology entrepreneurship space. I have partnered with three amazing individuals to form BUILDUP.vc a global impact firmed designed to connect, mentor and educate technology entrepreneurs.  

Along with BUILDUP, I’ve been writing, mentoring and hosting technology events since my last update.


If there is anything I can do to help you, please ask!
